Welcome to PlanEat Support!

PlanEat is your personal meal planning and grocery shopping assistant for iOS and Apple Watch. We're here to help you get the most out of the app.

About PlanEat

PlanEat helps you organize your meals and simplify grocery shopping with these features:

  • Recipe Management: Create, edit, and organize your favorite recipes
  • Meal Planning: Plan your meals on a visual calendar
  • Smart Shopping Lists: Auto-generate shopping lists from your meal plans
  • Category Organization: Organize ingredients by category for efficient shopping
  • Apple Watch App: Access your shopping list on your wrist
  • iCloud Sync: Keep your data synchronized across all your devices
  • Recipe Sharing: Import and export recipes with friends and family

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a recipe?

Navigate to the Recipes tab and tap the "+" button. Fill in the recipe details including ingredients, preparation steps, cooking time, and servings. You can also add tags to organize your recipes.

How do I plan meals?

Go to the Meal Planner tab to see your calendar. Tap on any date to add a meal. You can select one or more recipes, specify the meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snack), and set the number of people.

How does the shopping list work?

The shopping list is automatically generated from your planned meals. All ingredients from your meal plans are combined and organized by category. You can check off items as you shop, and the app remembers what you've bought.

Can I use PlanEat on my Apple Watch?

Yes! PlanEat includes a companion app for Apple Watch. You can view your shopping list and check off items directly from your wrist while shopping.

How do I sync my data across devices?

PlanEat uses iCloud to automatically sync your recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists across all your iOS devices. Make sure you're signed in to the same iCloud account on all devices and have iCloud enabled for PlanEat in your device settings.

How do I share recipes?

Open any recipe and tap the share button. You can export the recipe as a .planEat file and share it via Messages, Mail, AirDrop, or any other sharing method. Recipients can import the recipe directly into their PlanEat app.

What are Pro features?

PlanEat offers additional Pro features for enhanced meal planning and organization. Check the app for current Pro feature availability and early access opportunities.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All your data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never collect, access, or share your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual recipes, meals, or ingredients within the app at any time. To delete all local data, simply delete the app from your device. To remove iCloud data, go to Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage > PlanEat and delete the data.

System Requirements

  • iOS: Requires iOS 18.0 or later
  • watchOS: Requires watchOS 11.0 or later
  • iCloud: Optional, for sync across devices

Troubleshooting

My data isn't syncing

Check that:

  • You're signed in to iCloud on all devices
  • iCloud Drive is enabled in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud
  • PlanEat has iCloud access enabled in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud
  • You have sufficient iCloud storage available

The Apple Watch app isn't working

Make sure:

  • Your Apple Watch is paired with your iPhone
  • PlanEat is installed on both devices
  • Your devices are within Bluetooth range
  • Try force-quitting both apps and reopening them
