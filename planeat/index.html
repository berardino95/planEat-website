Millo

Your Personal Meal Planning Assistant

iOS watchOS
Download on the App Store

Millo is a native iOS and watchOS application designed to simplify meal planning and grocery shopping. Organise your recipes, plan your meals on a visual calendar, and generate smart shopping lists automatically.

AI Recipe Assistant

✦ Powered by Apple Intelligence

Millo harnesses Apple Intelligence to revolutionise how you manage recipes. Two intelligent modes make recipe creation effortless.

🪄

Generate Mode

Describe what you want to cook and Apple Intelligence creates a complete recipe — ingredients, quantities, and step-by-step instructions tailored to your language and measurement system.

📄

Convert Mode

Paste any recipe from a website, cookbook, or message and Apple Intelligence automatically converts it into a structured format with categorised ingredients and organised steps.

Requires iOS 26.0+ with Apple Intelligence enabled
