PlanEat

Your Personal Meal Planning Assistant

iOS & watchOS
Download on the App Store

PlanEat is a native iOS and watchOS application designed to simplify meal planning and grocery shopping. Organize your recipes, plan your meals on a visual calendar, and generate smart shopping lists automatically. Available for iPhone and Apple Watch.

✨

AI Recipe Assistant

Powered by Apple Intelligence

PlanEat harnesses the power of Apple Intelligence to revolutionize how you manage recipes. Two intelligent modes make recipe creation effortless:

🪄

Generate Mode

Describe what you want to cook, and Apple Intelligence creates a complete, personalized recipe with ingredients, quantities, and step-by-step instructions tailored to your preferences and local measurement system.

📄

Convert Mode

Paste any recipe from a website, cookbook, or message, and Apple Intelligence automatically converts it into a structured format with categorized ingredients, proper quantities, and organized cooking steps.

Available on iOS 26.0+ with Apple Intelligence enabled
